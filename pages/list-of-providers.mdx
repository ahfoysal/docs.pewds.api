# List of Providers

Pewds Api supports a wealth of API providers, which can be categorized into the following groups:




## News

[Prothom Alo:](https://www.prothomalo.com/) The Daily Prothom Alo is a daily newspaper in Bangladesh.

## Ecommerce

[Daraz:](https://www.daraz.com.bd/) Bangladesh's best online shopping store with 17+ million products at resounding discounts in dhaka, ctg & All across Bangladesh with cash on delivery.

[Birkoy.com:](https://bikroy.com/) Bikroy.com is a classified advertisements website operating in Bangladesh. 

[Foodpanda:](https://www.foodpanda.com.bd/) Order food from restaurants or groceries from top shops across Bangladesh. 


## Movies

[FlixHQ:](https://flixhq.to) Provides 1,000s of high-quality movies & series in high-definition online for free.


## Anime

[GogoAnime:](https://gogoanime.dk) The default provider for anime - one of the more well-known/established providers.
